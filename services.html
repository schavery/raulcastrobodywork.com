Feeling Sick?

Please notify me by phone with at least 24 hours notice if you are currently feeling sick, or have been sick within the past 7 days (ie. Flu, cold etc.) Please understand that your appointment may have to be rescheduled until any illness has passed. Likewise, please understand that if the massage therapist is feeling unwell we may have to postpone your appointment for a later date and time. This is to protect others who may utilize the work space from getting sick as well.
